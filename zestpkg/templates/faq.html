Frequently Ask Questions

Q. How to register for Solo Events?

Registering for Solo event is very simple, just after login follow these steps.

  1. Go to events page.
  2. Search for event you want to registered.
  3. Click on the event.
  4. Go to the bottom of page and Click Take Participation.
  5. You are done!

Q. How to register for Team Events?

Registering for Team event is little tricky, just after login follow these steps.

  1. Go to events page.
  2. Search for event you want to registered.
  3. Click on the event.
  4. Go to the bottom of page.
  5. Now you will see two options -
    • Create Team
      1. By clicking on Create Team you have to enter Team name
      2. After team name you are done, your team has been created and you are registered.
      3. But don't forget to share team code with your teammates so they can join you by following steps below.
    • Join Team
      1. By clicking on Join team you have to enter Team secret code
      2. Team code is a secret key you have to ask your friend, the one who created the team for secret code.
