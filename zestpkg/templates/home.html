Zest and Aamod is a cultural and sports fest held at SRMS CET , Bareilly every year. The motive of this events is to allow or provide freedom to the students to take part in various cultural and sports activities and showcase their talents .

In this students of not only this college but also of various other colleges from different places come and take part in various competitions and show their talents every year.

ABOUT SRMS TRUST

The memories of veteran freedom fighter, true Gandhian, ex-parliamentarian & ex minister of UP State, Shri Ram Murti Ji, were the inspiration for setting up the, Shri Ram Murti Smarak Trust in the year 1990.

The vision and mission of the Trust are to use all its resources for upliftment of mankind, by service to humanity. With the inspiration of Late Shri Ram Murti Ji and with the untiring zeal and enthusiasm of Shri Dev Murti, Shri Ram Murti Smarak College of Engg. & Tech. was established in 1996.The college today offers courses in Engineering, Pharmacy, Management and Computer Applications.

The SRMS Trust has also furthered a noble cause by establishing Shri Ram Murti Smarak Institute of Medical Sciences, which includes a 700-bed Multi Super Specialty; Tertiary Care hospital and Trauma Centre a Medical and a Nursing College. The campus of medical college and hospital is spread over an area of 30 acres and is located at 13.2 Kms. on the Bareilly-Nainital Highway, Bareilly (U.P).

ABOUT TYRO

TYRO – A Beginner, established in 1997,a panel of students comprising the very best amongst the best. The sole aim of Tyro is to bring about diversity of art, culture, academics and sports on a common platform.” It is dedicated to bring joy and jubilation by nurturing budding talent.

Tyro comes into full swing for the organization of a mega event – 'ZEST' & ‘AAMOD’. This is the platform wherein young and creative minds get to exhibit their talent through various competitions.

It’s time to extract the series of creativity from the best brains...So, herein, we extend our invitation to all, to be a part of this grand event.
