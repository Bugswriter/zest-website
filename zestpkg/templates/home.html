ABOUT THE EVENT

Zest and Aamod is an annual cultural and sports fest organized by Shri Ram Murti Smarak College of Engineering and Technology, Bareilly. The motive of this events is to allow or provide freedom to the students to take part in various cultural and sports activities and showcase their talents.

Zest & Aamod is the most awaited event of the entire college in which colleges across the state participate. All the winners and runner ups are provided with trophy's, Cash prize and Certificates.

Where activities like Solo and Duet Singing competitions, Solo and Group Dance competitions, gratis, charcolate, dumb charades and many other talent hunt competitions come under zest; for girls and boys separately, kho-kho, badminton(singles and doubles), Table tennis, volleyball, football, other athletic games are oraganised. Various stalls and fun games enhance the enthusiasm of the students and visitors.The highlight of the two day cultural - sports fest are the ranaissance (fashion show) and DJ night.
